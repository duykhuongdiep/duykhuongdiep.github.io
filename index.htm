    Ideal Job

    Job description

    A data analyst in general responsible for modelling, building and maintaining the organisation's database system. In addition, this position could assist the organisation by extract and analyse essential business data, helping stakeholders and managerment board giving appropriate business decisions. The ability of supporting the team as well as to create impacts on the organisation is what appealing about this role to me.

    Skill requirement

  • Appropriate qualification in computing or quantitative field.
  • Experience in business analysing and project managing.
  • Extensive knowledge in SQL (Structured Query Language) and SAS, be able to use automated tools to assist data management and maintenance.
  • Have a professional background from an organisation, with largely sophisticated methods of data management, in order to provide consultation on the current system.
  • Have a professional background from an organisation, with separate teams for data management and data analytics.
  • Have experience about R, AWS, Oracle and Tableau are favourable.

    • Current skills and further planning

    I currently have a Diploma in Information Technology with basic skill in SQL. To be able to achieve the position, I need to study for further qualifications, develop higher SQL and SAS skills, as well as study R, AWS, Oracle and Tableau. Furthermore, this position required a strong professional background, which must be obtained first by finding roles in organisations with already established data management system.
